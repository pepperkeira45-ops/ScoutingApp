WOBOT 141 Pit Scouting

Dashboard Training

Use the arrow keys or the buttons below to navigate this guide.

1. Logging In

  • Your account has already been created for you! Navigate to the app URL on your phone or tablet.
  • Email Format: [firstname]scout@wobot141.com
  • Password Format: [firstname]141 (all lowercase)
  • Example: If your name is Bob, your email is bobscout@wobot141.com and your password is bob141.

2. Finding Your Assignments

  • Once logged in, you will land on the "My Teams" dashboard.
  • The app automatically assigns you teams to scout based on who is available.
  • Track your personal progress bar to see how many teams you have left.
  • To start scouting, simply find a team on your list and tap the red INSPECT button.

3. The Pit Scouting Form

  • Robot Photo: Always start by taking a picture of the robot. The app compresses it automatically to save data.
  • Technical Specs: Ask the team about their Drivetrain, Fuel Intake, and Endgame Tower climbing capabilities (Level 1, 2, or 3).
  • The Interview: The final section is for Scouter Eyes Only. Ask the team what features they are proud of and what their "Pitch" is to an alliance captain.
  • Be Honest: Use the "Vulnerabilities" text box to record any private notes about exposed wiring or slow mechanisms.

4. Offline Mode & Syncing

  • Look at the top left of your screen. If you have Wi-Fi, it will say ONLINE.
  • If the venue Wi-Fi drops, it will change to OFFLINE. Keep scouting!
  • When offline, your reports are saved safely to your device's memory.
  • When you get connection back, a yellow UPLOAD SAVED DATA button will appear. Click it to sync your reports to the main database.
